What the passage gets right

Complete and relevant information

Appropriate word choice and tone

Clearly stated objectives

How can we improve this?

Font: make use of different text fonts and decorators to make invitees want to read the passage

Layout: have varying text sizes to emphasize importance of information, and have line spacing to make the overall passage look neater and more organized

Visual: use images that can give a hint as to what is expected of the event, to entice invitees to read the text

Contact Information: provide an actual number to add legitimacy and proper courtesy to the invitation, not just the names of the hosts

What would be the objective function?

The passage aims to invite guests for Robert and Alexandra's after-dinner party event. It does so in a formal yet festive tone. The invitation also encourages people who will be attending the event to coordinate with the hosts by June 1.
